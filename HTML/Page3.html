    • Solution

    Our solution for this problem is an app, which has a lot of benefits! The whole idea is based on a pointsystem where you get point for doing something good for the environment, for example public transport. When you take the bus, you pull out your phone to scan a QR-code (for example in the ticket/receipt or similar).

    When you collect a certain amount of points there will be given donations to the poor. The money will come from sponsors. They will get good reputation from donations, and also get ads on the app.

    The app will also contain a lot of achievements available for unlocking by reaching a certain amount of points, or by doing specific actions.

    Benefits for everyone!

    The app has a lot of great outcomes. It will first make the users do a lot of environmentally friendly actions. The outcome of this actions will mean money to the organisations, which will lead to more help to the poor. On top of everything this will be fun because of the achievements you will receive in the app.

    How does the application work?

    The application can be downloaded on your device very soon! It will support most of the softwares, and devices exisiting today.

    Helping Hand is connected to the camera on your device, and scans your receipts and tickets to give you points in the scoring system. You can also download a picture of your receipts and tickets from the camera roll if you’re not connected to the internet. When you’ve reached a certain amount of points, you’ll get a notification and can choose who you want to donate the money to. You can connect to Facebook and compete with your friends on who has been the most environmentally friendly. This can be seen on the scoreboard site in the app. This will make it fun to use the app, and with weekly challenges there’s always a reason to keep doing something for the environment.
