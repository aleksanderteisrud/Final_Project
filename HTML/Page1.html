In the modern world we’re living in it’s a shame that poverty still exists. Poverty increases the health risks, and when the lack of medications and knowledge is a big problem, the death rate will be extremely high. The last years we’ve taken a huge step with reducing the poverty world wide, but that doesn’t mean it’s completely gone. The poverty in South Asia, East Asia and Pacific is almost gone, but the poverty in Sub-Saharan Africa have actually increased since 1990, and it’s a big concern.
